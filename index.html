Frequently Asked Questions

How do i make an appointment

If you would like to make an appointment with any of our peactioners, please contact our receptionist staff. Alternatively you can book an appointment online. Every effort will be made to accomodate your preffered time and choice of practioner

How do i get a copy of my record

To get a copy of your record you can visit the hospital any day of the week and you'll be attended to by our health records office. If you're far away you can request for downloadable copy

Is there a charge for extra copies of records

If you have been our patient before or frequently attended to at our hospital there's no charges to made to get a copy

How do i know the right practioner

Depends on the kind of ailment or what you want to be attended to. Also once you visit the hospital or call the receptionist you'll be guided on proper practioner to book appointment with.
